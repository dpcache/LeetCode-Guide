Bitwise and Bit Shift Operators

>> is arithmetic shift right, >>> is logical shift right.
>>> is unsigned-shift; it'll insert 0. >> is signed, and will extend the sign bit.

Shift example:
01111111 >>> 2 = 00011111
10000000 >>> 2 = 00100000
01111111 >> 2 = 00011111
10000000 >> 2 = 11100000
